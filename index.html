Our Process

How We Curate Quality.

Every stone that passes through Diamond Corp. is evaluated against four foundational pillars — cut precision, clarity grading, colour consistency, and verified provenance. Each gem is independently certified, ensuring our clients receive not just beauty, but documented truth.

Optical Performance Review

We assess every diamond's light behaviour under controlled spectral conditions — evaluating brilliance, fire, and scintillation against international cut standards. Stones that fail our optical threshold are returned, not resold.

Certification & Documentation

Every stone is accompanied by grading reports from recognised independent laboratories. Documentation covers the 4Cs, fluorescence, polish, and symmetry. Full traceability records are issued alongside each purchase, forming a permanent stone identity file.

Sourcing Integrity

As a responsible diamond exporter, we partner exclusively with suppliers who comply with the Kimberley Process and maintain transparent supply chain disclosures. Origin disclosure is provided wherever available, with detailed sourcing notes appended to each stone's documentation.

Verification Standards

  • Independent third-party grading on every stone
  • Origin disclosure where supply chain permits
  • Full 4Cs grading report included with purchase
  • Pre-sale inspection notes on file
  • Kimberley Process compliance verified
  • Professional care & storage guidance issued
  • Fluorescence and symmetry data on record

Every document we issue reflects our commitment to transparent, accountable gem trading — from first inspection to final delivery.
