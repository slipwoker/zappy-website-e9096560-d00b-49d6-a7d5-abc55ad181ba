Questions.

If you don't find what you're looking for below, we welcome you to reach out directly. Every inquiry at Diamond Corp. is handled with the same care and precision we bring to every stone.

Are your diamonds certified?

Every diamond we offer is accompanied by a grading report from a recognized independent laboratory — most commonly the GIA or IGI. These certificates document the stone's cut, color, clarity, and carat weight, giving you complete confidence in your purchase.

How do you ensure ethical sourcing?

Transparency is foundational to our practice. We work exclusively with suppliers who comply with the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme and maintain full chain-of-custody documentation. We are happy to share provenance details for any stone upon request.

How are stone recommendations made?

Our specialists take time to understand your intentions — whether a gift, an heirloom, or an investment — before presenting curated options. Recommendations are guided by your preferences, budget, and the technical qualities that best serve your purpose, never by inventory pressure.

Do you offer custom or bespoke pieces?

Yes. We collaborate with master craftspeople to create entirely bespoke settings for our stones. From initial sketch to final polish, you are involved at every stage. Contact us to begin a consultation for a one-of-a-kind commission.

How should I care for my diamond jewelry?

Diamonds are remarkably durable, but settings and metals require attention. We recommend gentle cleaning with warm water and a soft brush, avoiding harsh chemicals. Store pieces individually in soft pouches to prevent scratching. Annual professional inspections keep prongs and settings secure.

What distinguishes Diamond Corp. from other diamond exporters?

Our reputation rests on decades of rigorous selection and an unwillingness to compromise on quality. As a trusted diamond exporter, we combine deep gemological expertise with a deeply personal approach — every client relationship is built on discretion, honesty, and long-term trust.
