Craft, Verified.

A modern approach to timeless materials.

Diamond Corp. was founded on a single conviction: that extraordinary stones deserve extraordinary scrutiny. From the moment a rough crystal leaves the earth, we trace its journey with the precision of a gemologist and the integrity of an independent diamond exporter—documenting provenance, evaluating cut geometry under magnification, and cross-referencing every certificate against international grading standards. Our origin is rooted in decades of working alongside master cutters and mine operators across four continents, building a supply chain defined not by volume, but by verifiable quality.

Premium, to us, is not a marketing word. It is a measurable condition: clarity grades confirmed under 10× loupe, colour assessed in standardised daylight-equivalent illumination, and provenance documented from rough to polished. Every stone that passes through our hands—whether a D-Flawless solitaire or a vivid fancy-colour sapphire—receives the same rigorous examination. We offer our clients not just a stone, but a complete picture of what they are acquiring: its history, its science, and its enduring value. That commitment to guidance is the foundation upon which Diamond Corp. has built its reputation.

Principles.

At Diamond Corp., every decision is guided by a set of enduring commitments. From the moment a stone enters our care to the final documentation that accompanies it, our standards remain unwavering — defining what it means to work with a trusted diamond exporter at the highest level.

Transparency

Every stone we handle is accompanied by complete provenance documentation, grading reports, and chain-of-custody records. We believe our clients deserve full clarity — no ambiguity, no hidden conditions, only verifiable facts presented with honesty.

Precision

Our inspection protocols adhere to the strictest gemological standards. Each diamond is evaluated under controlled lighting and magnification, with measurements and characteristics recorded to the finest decimal — because excellence is found in the details others overlook.

Stewardship

Precious stones are more than commodities — they are natural wonders formed over millennia. We handle every gem with the care it deserves: climate-controlled storage, anti-static packaging, and handling procedures that preserve integrity from acquisition through delivery.

Guidance

Our specialists bring decades of combined expertise to every client relationship. Whether navigating a complex acquisition or understanding grading nuances, we provide informed, impartial counsel — the kind of guidance that defines a lasting partnership in the diamond international corp tradition.
