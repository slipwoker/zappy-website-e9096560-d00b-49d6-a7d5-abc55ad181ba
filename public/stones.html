Our Verification Process.

Every stone that passes through Diamond Corp. undergoes a rigorous, multi-stage evaluation conducted by our expert gemologists. Our process is designed to document, assess, and contextualise each specimen with precision — providing complete transparency into the characteristics that define a stone's identity and quality.

01

Intake & Visual Review

Upon arrival, each stone is catalogued with a unique reference and subjected to an initial visual inspection under controlled lighting. Our gemologists assess surface characteristics, visible inclusions, colour zoning, and overall presentation — establishing a comprehensive baseline record before any further analysis begins.

02

Certification Check

Existing grading reports from recognised laboratories — including GIA, AGS, and HRD — are cross-referenced against the physical stone. Our specialists verify report authenticity, confirm laser inscription details where present, and note any discrepancies between documentation and observed characteristics to ensure complete accuracy of record.

03

Optical Performance Notes

Using precision light-performance tools and loupe examination, our team documents how each stone interacts with light — noting brilliance distribution, fire, scintillation patterns, and any optical anomalies. These observations are recorded as qualitative performance notes, offering a nuanced perspective that complements formal grading data.

04

Care & Handling Guidance

Each evaluation concludes with tailored care and handling recommendations specific to the stone's characteristics. Guidance covers appropriate cleaning methods, storage conditions, and sensitivity considerations — ensuring that every stone in our portfolio is maintained in optimal condition throughout its time with us.
